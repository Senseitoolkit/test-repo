The drummer's path

To become a well-rounded drummer rather than just memorize songs, a handful of grooves teach nearly every fundamental skill. Work through them roughly in order, and play each one slowly with rock-solid timing before pushing the tempo. Tap a lesson to open it; hit Load into sequencer to drop it into the kit above and start drilling.

Independence exercises

  • 1Keep the hi-hat constant and the snare on 2 & 4 — then move only the bass drum around underneath.
  • 2Then keep the bass drum constant and move the hi-hat accents instead.

Isolating one limb at a time is how true four-way coordination is built. Lesson 02 above is the drill for step one.

Timing checks

Practice any groove against each of these. They quickly reveal whether your internal clock is actually solid.

  • ·Quarter-note metronome (click on every beat)
  • ·Half-time click — only on 2 & 4
  • ·Click only on beat 4
  • ·Click only on beat 1, every other measure

Use the tempo drill above to start slow and ramp up once a groove feels locked.
