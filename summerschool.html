A summer of change

K1–G5 · two-week PYP summer school · exploring the UN Sustainable Development Goals through inquiry, Daily 5, embedded math, visual thinking routines, and turn-and-talk.

Central idea: The SDGs are a call for action for every human who cares about the future of the Earth.

Daily timetable

S1 09:00–09:40 S2 09:45–10:25 S3 11:10–11:50 S4 11:55–12:35 S5 13:35–14:15 S6 14:20–15:00

Weekly rhythm

3 inquiry days + 1 water-play day + 1 field-trip day. Flexible — slot the water and trip days wherever fits.

Two specials a day

PE, Art, ICT, Music, Library, Language — slot into any two of the six sessions.
