Diagnostic Testings - English and Maths

Diagnostic assessments for Reception through Year 6. Each year links to a dedicated app in the branch/ folder. Coming Soon

About these tests

Each test takes 10 minutes. Results show how many questions of each type the student completed and the overall totals. Use these totals to plan focused support and next steps.
